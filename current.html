DOPPLER
Project and Invoice Tracking Application

Doppler is a full stack application built for tracking and reporting in-house client projects and invoices. I've led this project as the sole developer from the ground up: from planning, to wirefaming and designing, to coding the front and backend. Doppler leverages MEAN stack technologies including Angular 7, Node.js, Express, and MongoDb along with Angular Material and Google Oauth.
